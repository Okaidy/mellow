At Mellow we invest in ideas, we consult and advise early stage companies, develop software and create brand identities. We take your business seriously, and ourselves not to seriously. We wear our hearts in our oft-wrinkled sleeves.

We are grateful for the companies who have trusted us, feel lucky to collaborate with such brilliant and amazing people. Can say we are proud of our project’s achievements.

Our approach isn’t exactly customary, and our partners don’t act the “traditional” way. Currently we invest in-kind thanks to our highly qualified team, becoming co-founders of new ideas.
